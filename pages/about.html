This website was made for the express purpose of cataloging mutliple articles of media, whether that be video games, old photgraphs, even NFTs. I believe in making everything on the internet permanent. If the blockchain fails, then no matter how...unpleasent...NFTs are to look at, they are still art. That's the important take-away with this site. I want people to realize that art is objectively anything that someone puts their minds to. Any kind of process of natural thought leads to art. This could be actual art, like a painting or an illustration. This could also be stuff like music and other things.
